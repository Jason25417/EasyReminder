# Privacy Policy · 隐私政策

**EasyReminder** does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

The app runs entirely on your device. Only when you explicitly act, it reads and
writes your **Reminders** (via Apple's EventKit) and the **.ics files you choose**
(via the system file picker). Nothing is sent to any server. There is no
analytics, no tracking, no account, and no third‑party SDK.

Reminders access is used solely to import into / export from your Reminders at
your request.

Questions: <https://github.com/Jason25417/EasyReminder/issues>

---

**EasyReminder** 不收集、不存储、不上传、不分享任何个人数据。

App 完全在你的设备本地运行：仅在你主动操作时，通过 Apple 的 **EventKit** 读写你的
**「提醒事项」**、以及你**明确选择的 .ics 文件**（经系统文件选择器）。不向任何服务器
发送数据，无分析、无追踪、无账号、无第三方 SDK。

「提醒事项」权限仅用于按你的操作导入到 / 导出自「提醒事项」。
